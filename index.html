Modelling the genetic structure
of a priori brain networks

This website displays analysis code and results from our pre-registered project modeling the genetic structure of a priori brain networks.
For more details on the hypotheses and analysis plan of this project refer to the pre-registration at https://osf.io/7n4qj

Analysis steps:

Code and results are displayed in conjunction.

  • Processing phenotypic data

  • Genetic quality control

  • Genome-wide association study for regional brain volumes

  • Evaluate GWAS

  • Processing genetic data

  • Descriptive statistics for genetic and phenotypic networks

  • Hypothesis 2 and 1: Comparison of genetic and phenotypic brain measures

  • Hypothesis 3: Genetic correlation between general cognitive abilities and brain networks

  • Genetic correlation between brain age and whole-brain shared morphology
